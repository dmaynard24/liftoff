FAQ

What is your design process like?

My process starts with understanding your goals and audience through research, followed by strategy, design exploration, refinement, and delivery. I keep you involved at every step.

What can clients expect in terms of a quote?

Every project is unique. After our initial conversation, I'll provide a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing based on your specific needs and scope.

What are your rates for service and do we get a refund?

Rates depend on the project scope. I offer flexible pricing models including per-project and monthly retainers. Refund policies are discussed during the contract phase.

What is your rate for creative services in total?

Total costs vary based on deliverables, timeline, and complexity. I'm happy to discuss your budget and find a solution that works for both of us.

What can you describe more on what you do for brands?

I help brands develop their visual identity, create cohesive design systems, build websites, and produce marketing materials that communicate their story effectively.
